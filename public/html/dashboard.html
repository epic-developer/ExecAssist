External Calendar Sync


Export/sync your tasks with your favorite calendar apps.



Having trouble?
Export your ExecAssist.io task feed in iCal format. In other words, you can link other calendar apps to display ExecAssist.io tasks with the Sync Task Feed button which uses a webcal. If your browser does not support webcals, try using your device's native browser (eg. Safari on iOS). If your device does not support the webcal standard, you can manually enter the iCal URL into a calendar app.

Unlike ExecAssist.io realtime sync across devices, calendars will sync/update at an interval that may be decided by the calendar app itself. ExecAssist.io will, however, prompt the calendar app to decrease this interval to 5 minutes or less.
